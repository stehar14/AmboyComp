Networking

We strive to develop a plan that's right for you.

Amboy Computer will ask the right questions to determine what are the best methods for you and your employees to share and protect your sensitive data internally and remotely, including LAN and LAN Management, WLAN (Wireless Connectivity), and Remote Access via a Virtual Private Network, Terminal Services or other remote methods.

Allow us to worry about the maintenance and support behind your network infrastructure. We work with existing infrastructures or can conduct an evaluation of your operations to propose a new design. Our job is to stay at the forefront of technology so that you don't have to.

Servers

Microsoft Server, Active Directory, and Exchange

Almost any sized business needs the central organization that Microsoft Server provides. Whether your organization needs enterprise solutions, a standard environment, Small Business Server or Windows Essentials, Amboy Computer can setup, install and configure your Active Directory Forest and Domain quickly and expertly to suit your needs.

We utilize the latest Microsoft Exchange server technologies including: Outlook and Outlook Web App; Outlook Anywhere and ActiveSync (for your portable devices) as well as legacy connections such as POP and IMAP. (Blackberry users are also supported.) Use Exchange’s Database Availability Groups (DAG) to replicate your data locally or remotely for little or no down-time.

Data Protection

Business Continuity Issues and Disaster Recovery

Allow Amboy Computer to protect your stuff with a Disaster Recovery Plan. Disaster Recovery is exactly what it sounds like... the worst thing ever. Our engineers can prepare your company for what you hope never happens. It's one thing to restore a file or folder from an accidentally deleted network share. It's another thing to lose everything and have to get it all back. It could take days - even weeks to get your organization back in business, and, as we all know, that time lost is revenue lost. Let Amboy Computer start preparing a custom Disaster Recovery Plan for you today. We know the right questions to ask and how to fit all of your needs into your budget.

HIPAA and HITECH: Planning Many of today’s businesses have to follow the HIPAA and HITECH guidelines, not only to protect their data, but to protect themselves. Our engineers can design your network to meet those requirements. Let us help protect your data today.

Network Protection, Anti-Virus and Virus Removal: To protect your network, we will examine your business' broadband connection, firewall, and network access. We will check to make sure that holes in your network are closed and that your Workstations, Servers, and appliances are all up-to-date with critical security patches and firmware revisions. Amboy Computer provides Preventative Corporate Anti-Virus Solutions as well as protection for home and remote users. For those infected by viruses or virus-like activity, we also provides expert Virus and Spyware Removal.

Storage and Virtualization: Virtualize your network for more flexibility. Expand on-the-fly; share storage; and create the high-availability you need that physical servers just can’t provide.
